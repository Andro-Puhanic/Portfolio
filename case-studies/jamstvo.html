Who are they?

Jamstvo Accounting is a long-standing family-run firm based in Rijeka, known for its trustworthiness, expertise, and friendly client approach. With decades of experience behind them, the company recognized the need to modernize its visual identity and create a stronger digital presence through a fully redesigned website.

How did we get here?

Communicating

Creating the conditions needed to uncover every detail required to translate their vision into something tangible. This stage laid the foundation for understanding not just what they wanted, but why those elements mattered to the project.

Understanding

Reviewing the meetings and questionnaires completed by the project leads. With carefully structured questions, I was able to extract the insights necessary to shape a solution that aligns with their expectations.

Creating

A long cycle of refinement, experimentation, and open communication between myself and the Jamstvo Accounting family. Through clear instructions on my side and their open feedback, we arrived at the final result.
