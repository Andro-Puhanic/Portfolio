What...?

The name ‘Svarog’ originates from the ancient Slavic deity, whose sons are fire and sun, symbols of life, strength, and illumination. Once a god of the Slavic pantheon, today a symbol of the past reborn as a movement. A movement that seeks to revive the forgotten treasures of the Balkans and weave them into a contemporary expression

My part in this masterpiece

Being part of the creation of the Svarog brand has been a genuine privilege and a deeply enriching professional experience. It was one of the first projects in which I was fully involved, and I had the opportunity to contribute to every essential stage of its development. From brand identity and marketing strategy to apparel graphics, illustrations, packaging design, typography, and the overall web experience.
